  • Q

    What is your minimum order quantity?

  • A

    We offer a low MOQ of 200 units per color/style for customization orders. For initial orders, we can go as low as 100 units per order. Any units falling below the MOQ will be subject to a surcharge.

  • Q

    What should I prepare to proceed with sampling?

  • A

    To proceed with sampling, you'll need to provide a tech pack, BOM, and artworks for your logo and printed/stripe pattern, if applicable. The artwork files should be in AI format with editable vector graphics. If you are not prepared with these required files, please contact us for further guidance. It's also important to provide fabric type and composition, as they are crucial for the sampling process. If you're uncertain about these details, you can submit a reference sample for us to assess the fabric quality.

  • Q

    What is a tech pack and BOM?

  • A

    A tech pack, or technical package, is a comprehensive blueprint that describes a product's design details. This conveys to a manufacturer how a garment is to be produced. It generally includes flat sketches, colorways, reference images, sewing instructions, labeling and packing information. The Bill of Materials, or BOM is a complete list of raw materials, components, and instructions required to produce a finished product, including quantities needed for manufacturing.

  • Q

    I am a novice in the industry. Can you offer some help?

  • A

    Absolutely! We understand that starting out in the industry can be challenging, but you can count on us to help you navigate the process. We can provide guidance and assistance in building your tech pack and specifications. Please don't hesitate to reach out to us for any questions or support you may need.
